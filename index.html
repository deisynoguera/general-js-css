Ipad 8

The iPad is a line of tablet computers designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc., which run the iOS and iPadOS mobile operating systems. The first-generation iPad was released on April 3, 2010; the most recent iPad models are the eighth-generation iPad, released on September 18, 2020; the fifth-generation iPad Mini, released on March 18, 2019; the third-generation iPad Air, released on March 18, 2019; and the fourth-generation iPad Pro, released on March 25, 2020.
